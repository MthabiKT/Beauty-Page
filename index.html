Routine and Health

The first step in a healthy hair routine is to wash and condition your hair correctly. It's essential to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Additionally, massaging the scalp while washing can help promote blood flow and improve hair growth. Furthermore, it's crucial to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all product buildup. Finally, a deep conditioner once a week can help maintain moisture and keep your hair healthy.
